Welcome to your interactive learning platform. This is a structured, self-paced environment where you can build real skills through guided roadmaps, hands-on topics, and progress tracking.

## What is this?

This platform combines documentation with interactive learning. Instead of reading static tutorials, you follow curated roadmaps that break complex subjects into manageable tracks and topics. As you work through each skill, you can mark your progress and pick up right where you left off.

## How it works

1. **Roadmaps** are high-level learning paths (e.g. Frontend Development). Each roadmap is divided into tracks.
2. **Tracks** group related topics together (e.g. JavaScript Fundamentals, CSS Basics).
3. **Topics** are individual lessons with explanations, code examples, and skills to practice.
4. **Skills** are checkboxes within each topic. Mark them complete as you go — your progress is saved to your account when signed in.

## Navigating the platform

- The **home page** shows all available roadmaps with an overview of your progress.
- Click a roadmap to see its tracks and topics laid out in order.
- Use the **sidebar** here in the docs to browse all content directly.
- Each topic page has **prev/next navigation** so you can move through a track sequentially.
