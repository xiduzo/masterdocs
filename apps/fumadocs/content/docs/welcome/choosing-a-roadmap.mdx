---
title: Choosing a Roadmap
description: How to pick the right learning path for you.
---

Roadmaps are the starting point for your learning journey. Each one covers a broad subject area and is broken down into tracks of related topics.

## Browse available roadmaps

Head to the [home page](/) to see all available roadmaps. Each card shows:

- The roadmap title and a short description
- A progress bar (if you're signed in) showing how far along you are

Click any roadmap card to open its detail page, where you'll see every track and topic laid out in order.

## What's inside a roadmap

Each roadmap is organized into **tracks** — logical groupings of topics. For example, the Frontend Development roadmap includes tracks like:

- JavaScript Fundamentals
- CSS Basics

Within each track, topics are ordered so that concepts build on each other. You're free to jump around, but following the suggested order will give you the smoothest experience.

## Starting your first topic

Once you've picked a roadmap, click into the first topic of the first track. Each topic page includes:

- An explanation of the concept
- Code examples you can study and experiment with
- Skills to check off as you master them
- Prev/next links to move through the track

Work through topics at your own pace. There's no timer and no pressure.
