---
title: Tracking Your Progress
description: How progress tracking works and how to get the most out of it.
---

The platform tracks your learning progress at the skill level, so you always know where you stand.

## How it works

Each topic contains one or more **skills** — specific things you should understand or be able to do after studying the material. Toggle a skill to mark it complete. Your progress is reflected in:

- The **progress bar** at the top of each topic page
- The **roadmap card** on the home page, which shows overall completion
- The **roadmap detail page**, where each track and topic shows its status

## Signing in

Progress is saved locally in your browser by default. To sync across devices and keep your data safe, sign in with your account using the button in the top navigation.

When signed in, your progress is stored on the server and will follow you wherever you log in.

## Picking up where you left off

When you return to the platform, your roadmap cards on the home page show exactly how far you've gotten. Click into any roadmap to see which topics still need attention — incomplete topics are easy to spot.

## Resetting progress

If you want to revisit material, just uncheck the skills you want to redo. There's no separate reset action — you have full control over each individual skill toggle.
