Pick a subject you want to learn, work through topics one at a time, and check off skills as you go. No deadlines, no pressure — just steady progress you can see.

## How it works

1. **Pick a roadmap** from the [home page](/) — each one is a complete learning path for a subject like Arduino or Figma.
2. **Work through topics** — each one teaches a focused concept with examples to study and experiment with.
3. **Check off skills** — mark what you've mastered. Your progress is saved and visible everywhere — on topic pages, roadmap cards, and the home page.

Sign in to sync your progress across devices. Otherwise it's saved in your browser.
